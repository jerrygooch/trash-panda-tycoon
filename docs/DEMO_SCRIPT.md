# Demo Script — Trash Panda Tycoon

A 60–90 second screen share walkthrough.

## 1. Main Menu (5s)
- Open Godot, click Play
- Title "Trash Panda Tycoon" with raccoon emoji
- Tagline: "Sort trash. Earn coins. Build an empire."
- Primary "Play" button is largest

## 2. Start Playing (10s)
- Click "Play"
- First round: extra mess capacity and slower spawns
- Items appear on conveyor belt
- HUD shows coins, timer, mess bar, level

## 3. Sorting (15s)
- Drag a banana peel into the Food bin → +1 coin, green flash
- Drag a pizza slice into the Food bin → +2 coins, combo counter appears
- Drag a soda can into the Metal bin → correct sort
- Combo meter builds — shows "x3" after consecutive correct sorts

## 4. Show Consequences (10s)
- Drag a plastic bottle into the Food bin → "Wrong!" red flash, mess increases
- Let an item fall off the bottom → mess increases
- At 70% mess, "High Mess!" warning appears
- At 85%+, "Mess Critical!" warning

## 5. Results Screen (10s)
- Round ends (timer or mess full)
- Shows: coins earned, correct/wrong/missed counts, best combo, empire level
- Missions section shows progress toward goals
- "Watch Ad x2" — click to double earnings (fake, works once per round)

## 6. Upgrade Screen (10s)
- Click "Upgrade"
- Shows 4 upgrades: Trash Value, Spawn Rate, Mess Capacity, Offline Earnings
- Each shows current level, next-level effect preview
- Buy an upgrade if affordable
- Click "Back" to return

## 7. Settings & Pause (10s)
- During a round, tap ⏸ pause button (top-right)
- Resume, Restart Round, Settings, Main Menu options
- Settings: Sound, Debug Overlay, Vibration (placeholder)

## 8. Continue & Save (10s)
- Return to Main Menu, click "Continue"
- Previous coins and upgrades are loaded
- Daily reward button available once per calendar day

## Key Features to Highlight
- Combo/streak system with milestone bonuses
- 5 missions (sort 25 items, 10 combo, 500 coins, 3 rounds, 1 upgrade)
- Offline earnings (coins accumulate while away, 2h cap)
- Empire level progression
- Procedural sound effects
- Conveyor belt animation
- Mobile-ready touch input (untested on real device)
